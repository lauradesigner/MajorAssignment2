EXERCISE

BENEFITS

EXERCISE IMPROVES MOOD

Daily physical activity stimulates various brain chemicals that may leave you feeling happier and more relaxed. Daily activities sometimes leave you stressed, and one way to release it is by going to the gym or simply jogging around your neighborhood. You may also feel better about yourself when you exercise regularly, which can boost your confidence.

EXERCISE PROMOTES BETTER SLEEP

Having difficulties sleeping? Regular physical activity can help you fall asleep and deepen your sleep. Don't exercise too close to bedtime, or you may be too energized to go to sleep.

EXERCISE MAKES YOU HEALTHY

Being active boosts high-density lipoprotein (HDL) cholesterol, the "good" cholesterol, and decreases unhealthy triglycerides. Exercise significantly reduces your risk of cardiovascular disease.
